# Self CheckOut at Safeway 

I went for a Safeway run for groceries right after school. Before entering I put my backpack and helmet in my cart, I then made my way through doors inside Safeway. Afteringm scanning some of my items for deals on the app so that they can be applied when I check out I choose the self-checkout line as I had few items.

When I got to first got to the machine, the prompt spoke to me and said to scan a membership card if I had one. I was confused at first because I didn't have one and wihtout it the deals woudl be able to transfer from my app. The **mental model** was throw off. I thought I would scan my app on the machine for it to read my account. After a worker helped me figure that out that all I need ot do was input my number I got that done.

Then it was after I started scanning my items that I become comfortable because it felt familiar this is a good exmaple of **feedback**. It was because of the beep i had after I scacnned each item that I knew I was doing it right. Especially because that's how I’ve seen cashiers scan items

I was confused what could possibly be in there but it was my backpack nad helemt. The system was trying to help but made it worse but a worker there saw my situation and cleared it for me and I was able to complete the checkout.
